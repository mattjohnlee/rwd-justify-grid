RWD Justify Grid

A responsive and semantic SASS grid partial by Matt Lee. Inspired by the article Text-align: Justify and RWD, by Patrick Kunka of Barrel. Grid use and mixins inspired by the excellent Stubble starter kit from Bearded.

The Grid

These bars show the underlying 12-column grid across all breakpoints. Column widths are set to a percentage of the total width (in this case, 6%), and gutter widths are calculated automatically by the text-align:justify property. To make wider columns and narrower margins, just increase the $columnwidth variable in the grid partial. If you want a gutterless grid, divide 100% by your total number of columns. In the case of a 12-column grid, $columnwidth: 8.3333% would make a gutterless grid.
